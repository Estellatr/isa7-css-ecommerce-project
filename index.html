About Us

Palette was first founded by Jane Doe (artist and illustrator) in 1997.
It was founded with the goal of providing an inclusive space for aspiring and established artists to sell and display their works. Since it's early days, the small shop has grown exponentially and is now a combined gallery and store, home to the works of many artists from around the world. The works come in all shapes and sizes, ranging from oil paintings and prints, to ceramics and jewellery.
Palette also hosts several events to encourage art and self-expression in the local community, with the goal to "help people to create". These include; weekly illustration classes, painting classes, and even monthly children's workshops. Read more about our classes here. Book a tour of our gallery via our online-shop.
